Review of books

  • Flowers For Algernon
  • Pet Sematary
  • World War Z

Feelings

I love all three books, the one that I had an emotional breakdown is Flowers For Algernon. Amazing journey, amazing transformation and downfall, the book will forever stay in my heart.

Pet SEMETARY is my latest read, my first Stephen King and woah, a heavy subject. Made me stop several times during the reading because I needed to take a break.

World War Z flips the world upside down. People of once importance become those of less importance, in a world where zombies roam the streets and what a trip, a terrifying and deep look into the world during crisis.
